Healthcare Patient Portal

Scroll through the prototype inside the device frame

The Challenge

The client, a multi-location hospital network, struggled with patient overload, manual appointment management, and increasing phone support demand. Patients couldn't access lab results or medical records unless they visited the hospital physically.

The Transformation

Cypher Digital built a secure healthcare portal that allows patients to book appointments, view records, consult doctors online, and manage prescriptions — all from home.

  • Online appointment scheduling with automated reminders
  • HIPAA-inspired secure record access & encrypted authentication
  • Telemedicine chat + video consultation
  • Role-based dashboard (Doctor / Patient / Admin)

Measured Outcomes

Real operational impact after launching the healthcare portal

Metric Before After Impact
Appointment Booking Time 12 minutes (phone) 1.5 min (self-service portal) -87%
No-show / Missed Appointments 28% 6% -78%
Phone Call Support Volume ~300 calls/day ~80 calls/day -73%
Patient Satisfaction Score 3.4 / 5 4.8 / 5 +41%

Final Impact

The hospital shifted from paper-first operations to a seamless digital workflow. Patients spend less time waiting — and more time receiving care.

“Patients can schedule, consult, and access medical records from anywhere. It transformed the patient journey.”
Head of IT — Hospital Client
