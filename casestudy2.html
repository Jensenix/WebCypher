From Fragmented Journeys to Connected Care

Patients needed a single, trusted place to schedule, consult virtually, access results, and message clinicians—without sacrificing privacy.

Before our engagement, patients juggled multiple apps and phone calls just to confirm an appointment or retrieve lab results. Staff were overwhelmed by manual intake, repetitive questions, and incomplete charts. Telehealth was available, but disjointed from records and billing, causing delays and drop-offs.

What We Changed

  • Unified portal for appointments, telehealth, secure messaging, and billing.
  • EHR integration (FHIR/HL7) to sync labs, medications, allergies, and visit notes.
  • Role-based privacy and encryption to meet HIPAA requirements end-to-end.
  • Accessible UI (WCAG 2.1 AA) with bilingual support for key flows.

The result is a calm, predictable care journey. Patients complete pre-visit triage from home, join video visits in a click, and receive follow-up tasks automatically. Staff see fewer calls, fewer no-shows, and cleaner data.

Clinical Impact

By unifying scheduling, telehealth, and records into one experience, the portal reduced friction for patients and workload for staff. Automated reminders and pre-visit triage improved preparedness, while integrated results and care plans boosted follow-through.

Most importantly, the system built confidence: patients felt informed and in control; clinicians trusted the data pipeline; and administrators saw measurable gains in efficiency and satisfaction.
