Performance Matters: Speed As a Product Feature

By Cypher Team • Nov 2024 • 6 min read

Speed changes behavior. Every 100ms of latency compounds drop-off across funnels. Treat performance as a core feature and it will pay back across SEO, conversion, and retention.

Optimize what users feel first: LCP, TTI, and input delay. Pre-render critical paths, cache at the edge, and use streaming where possible. Measure regressions on every PR.

Perceived performance matters too. Skeletons, optimistic UI, and instant feedback maintain momentum even when the network can’t.

Great products are fast by default—and resilient when things aren’t.
