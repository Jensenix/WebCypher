E-Commerce Scale & Fast Growth

Scroll through the showcase inside the device frame

The Challenge

The client operated a fast-growing online store but couldn’t scale profitably. Pages loaded slowly, conversion plateaued, and ads became expensive. Average cart value was low, and repeat purchases were nearly zero.

  • Mobile checkout abandonment reached 68%
  • Paid ads became more expensive with declining ROAS
  • Inventory & fulfillment manually managed through spreadsheets

The Transformation

Cypher Digital redesigned the entire buying experience focused on performance and revenue optimization.

  • Ultra-fast storefront → rebuilt using Next.js + server side rendering
  • 1-click checkout & auto-apply vouchers
  • Smart recommendations → increase Average Order Value (AOV)
  • Email automation abandoned cart → recovered revenue
  • Analytics event tracking integrated with Pixel + GA4

Measured Outcomes

Here are the results after 90 days

Metric Before After Impact
Conversion Rate 1.8% 4.9% +172%
Average Order Value (AOV) $42 $87 +107%
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) $26.20 $13.40 -49%
Returning Customers 9% 31% +244%

Final Impact

The store shifted from “spend ads to survive” → to profitable growth mode. Automation and analytics allowed the team to operate with data, not guesswork.
