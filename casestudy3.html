Scaling beyond limits

During flash sale events, traffic spiked by 30x within 60 seconds. Their previous architecture crashed once traffic exceeded just **2,500 concurrent visitors**.

Cypher Digital re-engineered the platform using:

  • AWS Autoscaling + CloudFront Edge CDN
  • Microservices + Queue-based traffic distribution
  • Lazy loading + SSR for first paint optimization

Result? The platform supported 120,000+ shoppers with zero downtime.
